About me

Hello! My name is Ilja Tkachuk (also known as timCF). I'm backend software developer, live in Tallinn, Estonia.

I have been programming since 2012. At the beginning I had some experience of development in imperative languages like C++ and JavaScript, but I did well only when I switched to functional languages like Erlang and Elixir.

Today my main skill is functional backend programming and I'm strong expert in BEAM/Erlang VM platform, Erlang and Elixir languages. I have strong understanding of multithreading and interaction between Erlang processes. Of course in commercial development I'm using mainstream frameworks like OTP, Phoenix and Ecto.

Future plans - to become more familiar with static-typed functional languages like Haskell. If static-typed languages for BEAM/Erlang VM (like Elchemy or Alpaca) will be successful - I will be happy to switch.
